## Positioning

**Core hook:** AI that generates a visual future self — not just a generic mood board.  
**Differentiator:** Personalized vision boards built around the user's selfie and goals; no competitor leads with "AI" in the title in this niche.  
**Emotional angle:** "Future Self" — the psychological trigger that drives click-through above generic "vision board" apps.
